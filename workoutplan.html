3-Month Workout Plan for Posture and Full Body Strength

Improve your posture and full body strength with this 3-month workout plan. Each day focuses on different muscle groups and includes mobility work to help with alignment and strength.

Month 1: Foundation Phase

Day 1: Upper Body & Core
  • Push-up: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps per arm
  • Dumbbell Chest Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dead Bug: 3 sets of 10 reps per side
Day 2: Lower Body & Core
  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Dumbbell Deadlifts: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Side Plank: 3 sets of 20 seconds per side
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Bird Dog: 3 sets of 10 reps per side
Day 3: Full Body Mobility & Posture
  • Cat-Cow Stretch: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Cobra Stretch: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Wall Angels: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Child's Pose: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Face Pulls (with resistance band): 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Thoracic Extensions (foam roller): 3 sets of 10 reps

Month 2: Strength Phase

Day 1: Upper Body & Core
  • Incline Push-ups: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 4 sets of 10 reps per arm
  • Dumbbell Chest Press: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank with Shoulder Taps: 4 sets of 10 taps per side
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Dead Bug: 4 sets of 10 reps per side
Day 2: Lower Body & Core
  • Goblet Squat: 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Deadlifts: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Glute Bridges with March: 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Side Plank with Leg Raise: 4 sets of 20 seconds per side
  • Walking Lunges: 4 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Bird Dog with Elbow-to-Knee: 4 sets of 10 reps per side
Day 3: Full Body Mobility & Posture
  • Cat-Cow Stretch: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Cobra Stretch: 4 sets of 30 seconds
  • Wall Angels: 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Child's Pose with Side Reach: 4 sets of 30 seconds per side
  • Face Pulls with Resistance Band: 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Thoracic Extensions with Rotation: 4 sets of 10 reps per side

Month 3: Power and Mobility Phase

Day 1: Upper Body & Core
  • Push-up to Downward Dog: 5 sets of 10 reps
  • Renegade Rows (plank position with dumbbells): 5 sets of 8 reps per arm
  • Dumbbell Chest Press: 5 sets of 8 reps
  • Plank with Arm Reach: 5 sets of 10 reaches per arm
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 5 sets of 8 reps
  • Dead Bug with Weight: 5 sets of 8 reps per side
Day 2: Lower Body & Core
  • Bulgarian Split Squat: 5 sets of 8 reps per leg
  • Single-leg Deadlift: 5 sets of 8 reps per leg
  • Hip Thrust with Dumbbell: 5 sets of 10 reps
  • Side Plank with Hip Dip: 5 sets of 20 seconds per side
  • Lateral Lunges: 5 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Bird Dog with Resistance Band: 5 sets of 8 reps per side
Day 3: Full Body Mobility & Posture
  • Cat-Cow Stretch with Deep Breathing: 5 sets of 10 reps
  • Cobra Stretch: 5 sets of 30 seconds
  • Wall Angels: 5 sets of 15 reps
  • Child's Pose with Side Reach and Hold: 5 sets of 30 seconds per side
  • Face Pulls with Resistance Band: 5 sets of 15 reps
  • Thoracic Extensions with Deep Rotation: 5 sets of 10 reps per side
